About The Company

Founded with a vision to redefine luxury, Capella is a premium jewelry brand that specializes in crafting exquisite, high-end pieces that stand out for their originality, rarity, and exceptional craftsmanship. From conceptual design to final polishing, we maintain uncompromising standards of quality and detail.

With headquarters in San Francisco and regional offices across North America, Europe, and Asia, we we serve a discerning clientele who seek not just jewelry, but heirlooms — pieces that carry stories, evoke emotion, and hold enduring value.
